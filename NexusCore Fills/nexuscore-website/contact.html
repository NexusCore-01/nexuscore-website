Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and processes

What is your typical project timeline?
Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple websites typically take 2-4 weeks, while complex applications can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.
Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?
Yes, we offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages to ensure your solution remains up-to-date, secure, and performs optimally.
What technologies do you work with?
We work with a wide range of technologies including React, Angular, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, .NET, and various cloud platforms like AWS and Azure.
How do you handle project communication?
We maintain regular communication through weekly status meetings, project management tools, and are always available via email or phone for urgent matters.
Do you provide hosting services?
While we don't provide hosting directly, we help you choose the best hosting solution and handle the deployment process for you.
Do you work with international clients?
Yes! We serve clients worldwide and are experienced in working across different time zones. We use collaborative tools to ensure smooth communication regardless of location.
